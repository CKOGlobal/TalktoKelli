Fill it out honestly. The more real you are with me on that form, the more value you're going to get from our call. I'll show up already in your corner, already thinking about your specific situation.

This is your path. This is your wealth-building journey. I'm just here to make sure you don't miss your exit.

Ready? Let's go.

## Production Notes for HeyGen

- **Pacing**: Conversational, not rushed. Natural pauses after each 2-3 sentence block.
- **Emphasis**: "YOUR road trip", "YOUR journey", "riding shotgun" — lean into these
- **Smile cue**: Genuine smile on "Like, genuinely. This is exciting."
- **Energy shift**: Slightly more serious/grounded on "I know where the shortcuts are..."
- **Closing**: End on energy — "Let's go." should feel like a sendoff, not a goodbye
- **Background**: Clean, neutral — not distracting. Warm tones work well.
- **Length target**: 70-80 seconds
